Top 5 Challenges Faced by HoReCa Businesses in Purchasing Raw Materials

SaravanaKumar

CO-Founder -CSO

22 March 2022

2mins Read

The HoReCa industry (hotels, restaurants, and cafes) in India is a rapidly growing sector, with a high demand for quality raw materials. Purchasing raw materials is an essential part of any HoReCa business, and it can be challenging to get it right. In this blog post, we will explore the top five challenges faced by HoReCa businesses in purchasing raw materials and how they can overcome these challenges.

1. Quality assurance

The first and foremost challenge that HoReCa businesses face when purchasing raw materials is ensuring the quality of the products they purchase. Raw materials are the foundation of any HoReCa business, and the quality of these materials has a direct impact on the quality of the end product. Therefore, it is essential to source raw materials from reliable suppliers who can provide the required quality. However, it can be challenging to assess the quality of raw materials without proper testing, and this can lead to a situation where the end product does not meet customer expectations. To overcome this challenge, HoReCa businesses need to establish a robust supplier evaluation process and test the raw materials before using them in the production process.

2. Price volatility

Another major challenge in raw material procurement is price volatility. The prices of raw materials can fluctuate significantly due to market conditions, seasonal variations, and other factors. This can lead to a situation where HoReCa businesses have to pay higher prices for the raw materials they require, which can impact their profit margins. To overcome this challenge, HoReCa businesses need to establish a robust price monitoring process and negotiate with suppliers to secure the best possible prices.

3. Supply chain disruptions

HoReCa businesses face significant supply chain disruptions when it comes to purchasing raw materials. The supply chain for raw materials is often long and complex, involving multiple suppliers, distributors, and transporters. Any disruption in this supply chain can lead to a shortage of raw materials, delayed deliveries, and increased costs. To overcome this challenge, HoReCa businesses need to establish contingency plans to deal with supply chain disruptions and maintain a buffer stock of raw materials to ensure uninterrupted production.

4. Regulatory compliance

The procurement of raw materials for HoReCa businesses is subject to various regulatory requirements, including food safety standards, import/export regulations, and environmental standards. Ensuring compliance with these regulations can be challenging, and failure to do so can lead to legal and financial consequences. To overcome this challenge, HoReCa businesses need to stay updated on the regulatory requirements and ensure that their suppliers comply with them.

5. Inventory management

HoReCa businesses need to manage their inventory efficiently to ensure that they have the right quantity of raw materials at the right time. However, managing inventory can be challenging, especially when it comes to perishable raw materials such as fresh produce. Overstocking can lead to wastage, while understocking can lead to production delays and customer dissatisfaction. To overcome this challenge, HoReCa businesses need to establish a robust inventory management system that takes into account factors such as demand forecasting, lead times, and shelf life.

Conclusion

In conclusion, raw materials procurement is a critical aspect of HoReCa businesses in India, and it is not without its challenges. To overcome these challenges, HoReCa businesses need to establish robust supplier evaluation processes, monitor prices, maintain buffer stocks, ensure regulatory compliance, and manage their inventory efficiently. By doing so, they can ensure a steady supply of quality raw materials and maintain their competitive edge in the market.
